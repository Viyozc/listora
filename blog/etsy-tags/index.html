How Many Tags on Etsy? The 13-Tag Strategy That Actually Ranks

Updated 2026-07-22 · Listora team

Why 13 tags matter more than your title

Etsy matches a shopper's multi-word search against your tags first. A search for "boho wedding invite template" is compared to your tags phrase-by-phrase. If you've left tags empty or used single words, you simply don't appear. All 13 tags are 13 free rankings — most sellers waste at least half.

Turn each tag into a phrase, not a word

"Planner" is too broad to rank for and too competitive. "daily planner printable" matches a real, high-intent query. Use all 13 tags as 1–3 word phrases that a buyer would actually type. A free listing SEO audit shows which of your 13 slots are single words costing you impressions.

Cover the variants buyers use

The same product is searched many ways: "wedding planner", "bridal planner", "marriage planner printable". Spread those variants across separate tags instead of repeating one. Each variant is a different search you can win. Don't cannibalize by stuffing the same phrase into five tags.

Don't stuff the title into the tags

Etsy already reads your title. Repeating title words in tags is redundant — Etsy down-weights it. Use tags for the phrases that didn't fit the title. The audit's keyword-coverage score reveals gaps between title, tags, and description.

Renew strategically, not blindly

Renewing re-orders your listings in search but doesn't improve relevance. Save renewals for your worst performers after you've fixed their tags. Batch-optimize first, renew second — optimization beats churn.
